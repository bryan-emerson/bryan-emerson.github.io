Open the console to play WAR!


1. The game is now live and the first hand has been dealt.
2. you can see the cards played for each hand, who won this hand, and how many cards each player has after the hand has been played.
3. To play the next hand type: `playRound(playerOneDeck, playerTwoDeck);`
4. The console will display the cards played, the winner of the hand, and how many cards each player has left.
5. If a tie is dealt, war will be declared and a subsequent hand will be dealt!
6. The first player to collect all cards wins!
